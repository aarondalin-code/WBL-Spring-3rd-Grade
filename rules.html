Game Format
  • Field Dimensions: 60 feet bases, 46 feet pitching mound.
  • Game Length: 2:00 or max 6 innings.
    • Official Game: After 4 innings (or 3.5 if home team is winning) due to weather.
    • No New Inning: After 1:45. Hard stop at 2:00.
    • Inning Starting at 1:45: Is the final inning.
    • Time Expiration: If game stops due to time, score reverts to last completed inning.
  • Run Limit: 5 per half inning, unlimited in final inning.
  • Mercy Rule: Not in effect.
  • Substitution Rule: Every player sits one inning before another player sits twice.
  • No Player: Should sit two innings in a row.

Batting Rules
  • Roster Batting: Everyone who shows up is in the lineup.
  • Lineup Rotation: Coaches should rotate order weekly.
  • Late Arrivals: Placed at bottom of order.
  • Thrown Bats: One warning per team; second offense results in batter out.

Pitching Rules
  • Distance: 46 feet.
  • Pitch Limit: Max 75 pitches or 3 innings.
  • Warmups: 8 in 1st inning, 5 thereafter.
  • Hit by Pitch: 3 in an inning = pitcher removed.
  • Coach Pitch Option: After 4 walks in an inning.
    • Coach must throw overhand from 46 feet.
    • Player remains in fielding position.
    • Player may return if limits not reached.
    • Coach may not start an inning.
  • Mound Visits: 1 per pitcher per inning.
