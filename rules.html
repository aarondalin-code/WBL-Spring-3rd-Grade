Game Format Time, innings, run limit
‚åÑ

  • Length: 2:00 or max 6 innings

Pitching Limits, coach pitch rules
‚åÑ
  • Kids must pitch from 46 feet
  • Max of 75 pitches per game or 3 innings
  • Warm up pitches: 8 in 1st inning, then 5 after
  • Hit by pitch: if 3 kids are hit in an inning, pitcher must be removed
  • If a kid walks 4 in an inning, coach has option to pitch remaining inning to keep game moving
  • Coach must throw overhand and pitch from 46 feet
  • Kids will continue to field the pitcher position while a coach is pitching
  • If that kid hasn‚Äôt reached his pitch/inning limit, he may be brought back in
  • Coach will not ever start an inning
  • Mound visits: coaches allowed 1 per pitcher per inning

Fielding 10 fielders option, errors, play stops
‚åÑ
  • Coaches can decide to play 10 total fielders with 4 outfielders (no short fielder)
  • Teams may play with a minimum of 8 players in the field
  • Errors: players may advance a max of 1 base, and defense has a free shot to get that baserunner out
  • If an outfielder overthrows an infielder, baserunners get a max of 1 base, except runner on 3rd (see Locked at 3rd rule)
  • Play ends when ball is returned back to pitcher‚Äôs mound
  • Coaches should play kids in both infield and outfield each game: every player should play 2 innings in infield and 2 in outfield

Special Rules Bunting, tagging, 3rd strike, locked at 3rd
‚åÑ
