WBL 3rd Grade 2026

Rules
Game Format ▾
  • Field Dimensions: 60 Feet Bases, 46 Feet Pitching Mound
  • Game Length: 2:00 or max 6 innings
    • Game is official after 4 innings or 3.5 if home team is winning (weather)
    • No new inning after 1:45, hard stop at 2:00
    • Any inning starting at 1:45 is the last inning
    • If the score of the game is stopped because of the time limit, scores get reverted to last full inning
  • Run Limit: 5 per half inning, unlimited in last inning
  • Mercy Rule: 15 run lead after 4 innings
  • Minimum Play: 2 defensive innings + 1 at bat
  • Batting Order: Everyone bats, continuous lineup
  • No On Deck: No on deck batters
  • Warmups: pre-game warmups must stay off infield once teams begin infield warmups
Equipment ▾
  • Metal cleats are not allowed
  • Bat Rules: USA Bats only (no USSSA)
  • Helmet: helmets are required for all batters and base runners
  • Catchers must wear full gear (mask, chest protector, shin guards)
  • Parents: please label all equipment with players name
Batting ▾
  • 3 strikes = out
  • Walks: max 2 walks per inning, then coach pitch starts (same at bat)
  • Coach Pitch: coach gets 3 pitches. If the player still doesn’t hit the ball, the player is out.
  • No bunting
  • No infield fly rule
Pitching ▾
  • Pitchers must pitch from 46 feet
  • Pitch Count: Max of 75 pitchers per game or 3 innings
    • Pitch Smart Guidelines
  • Warmups: players will get 8 warm up pitches in 1st inning, 5 for innings after that
  • A pitcher can only pitch 3 consecutive innings (max)
  • A pitcher can return to the mound after being removed, but only if it is not the same inning he was removed
    • may be brought back in to start the next inning.
  • Pitcher rest rules
    • 1 inning pitched = 0 days rest
    • 2 innings pitched = 1 day rest
    • 3 innings pitched = 2 days rest
  • Hit batters: max 2 hit batters per inning, then coach pitch starts (same at bat)
  • No balks
  • No intentional walks
  • No throwing curveballs, sliders, or changeups
Fielding ▾
  • 10 players on the field (4 outfielders)
  • Infielders must stay in the dirt until the ball is hit
  • No outfielder can be positioned in the infield (including short right/left field)
  • Outfielders must stay in the grass until the ball is hit
  • Teams can position their outfielders how they want, but must stay in the outfield
  • No shift rule (2 infielders must be on each side of 2nd base)
  • No “rover” / extra infielder allowed
  • Ball hits pitcher: ball is dead, batter gets 1st base (no runners advance unless forced)
  • Infield fly rule does not apply
Catching ▾
  • Catchers must wear full gear (mask, chest protector, shin guards)
  • No dropped third strike rule
  • Catchers must be behind the plate, in the catcher’s box
  • Pitchers may not pitch and catch in the same game (safety)
Base Running ▾
  • No leading off
  • No stealing
  • Runners can advance on passed balls only when coach pitch is in effect
  • Slide rule: players must slide to avoid contact at bases
  • No headfirst slides
Special Rules ▾
  • Coach Pitch: once coach pitch starts, players can advance on passed balls
  • Overthrow rule: runners can advance 1 base on an overthrow if the ball goes into dead ball territory
  • Infielders can tag runners (no “must slide” unless contact)
  • Players should not throw their bats (warning first, then out)
  • Timeouts: coaches should limit mound visits / timeouts
  • Illegal pitch types not allowed (curveballs, sliders, changeups)
Playoffs & Consolation ▾
  • Top 6 make championship playoffs. Seeds 1–2 receive byes.
  • Round 1: Seed 3 vs 6; Seed 4 vs 5.
  • Semis: Winner(4/5) plays Seed 1; Winner(3/6) plays Seed 2.
  • Championship: Winners of semis.
  • Seeds 7–10 (Round 1: 7v10, 8v9; plus follow-on games per your bracket).
  • Winning Percentage (Win%). (Wins + 0.5 × Ties) ÷ (Wins + Losses + Ties).
  • Head-to-Head (two-team tie only). If exactly two teams are tied in Win%, the higher seed goes to the team that won the head-to-head matchup (only if that game did not end in a tie).
  • Run Differential (overall). Runs For − Runs Allowed.
  • Runs For (overall). Higher total Runs For.
  • Runs Allowed (overall). Lower total Runs Allowed.
  • Alphabetical Order. If still tied after all steps above, seeding is determined alphabetically by team name.
Coaching ▾
  • Only coaches allowed on the field during play are 1st and 3rd base coaches
  • Coaches should encourage sportsmanship and respect
  • Issues: Please report any issues to league director
Sportmanship ▾
  • Umpires/Plays on the field
    • No arguing of balls and strikes by players, coaches or parents/fans
    • Cadet umpires, bad calls are expected
    • Only head coaches may talk to the umpires
    • If a player argues with an umpire, that player is to be removed from the game and has to sit a mandatory 2 innings in a row.
    • If parents are arguing or heckling an umpire, they will be asked to leave field and sit in car. There will be no exceptions to this rule.
    • If coaches argue with umpires, they will be reported to the WBL Board for discipline
  • Players: Should stop cheering or banging on fence when opposing pitcher starts his windup
    • Violation of this rule could result in your teams batter being called out
  • Handshake: Players will line up and shake hands/fist bumps after each game. Please encourage the players to say “Good Game” or something similar while shaking hands.
  • Dugouts: players must clean up after themselves and leave a clean dugout for the next game
Weather/Game Cancellations ▾
  • Fields: If fields are not playable, the league director will notify all coaches
  • Game cut short: If games are interrupted and stopped due to bad weather and the game is not yet official (see Game Length above), the game will not count. However, if both coaches want to continue that game during a practice time slot, that is allowed. Both coaches need to be in full agreement if the game is to be continued.
  • Rescheduling: The practice schedules of all teams are posted on the Team Page. Full coaches contact information is posted on the Contacts page. Please reach out and try your best to re-schedule your game during one of your teams practice times during the week. All teams may not be able to do this, so that is why we are using win% to determine playoff seedings.
